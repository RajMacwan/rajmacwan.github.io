Current Research

Exploring the privacy side of infrastructure.

To understand security and privacy deeply, I build. Here's what I'm running right now.

🧅 LIVE · TOR HIDDEN SERVICE

DarkPulse Security

A privacy-first cybersecurity news aggregator operating as a Tor hidden service. Built on a Whonix-isolated infrastructure with full operational separation. Aggregates 18+ public feeds, maintains the CISA KEV mirror, tracks ransomware groups, and publishes weekly long-form case files.

Why build it? Because understanding adversarial tradecraft requires operating in the same threat model it assumes. This is an engineering exercise in end-to-end privacy, not a product.

Operated under the pseudonym TheMacwan — a separate operational identity kept intentionally distinct from my professional profile.
