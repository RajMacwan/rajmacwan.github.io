Is it just me, or is it broken?

A live status board for the SaaS, cloud, AI, identity, payments and dev-comms services people actually ask me about. Polled directly from each vendor's official Statuspage feed — no crowdsourced noise, no scraping. If it says down, the vendor itself is saying down.

The board populates on the first cron run. Auto-refresh every 10 minutes via GitHub Actions.

What this is — and what it isn't

What it is: a public-utility dashboard. The same call I make to Stripe's /api/v2/summary.json is the call Stripe themselves use to power their status.stripe.com banner. There's no middleman.

What it isn't: a substitute for Downdetector. Telcos (Verizon, AT&T, T-Mobile), banks, and most consumer apps don't publish a Statuspage feed, so they're not on this board yet. A crowdsourced telco/banking tier is on the roadmap once licensing is figured out.

How to read it: 🟢 operational · 🟡 degraded (some users affected) · 🟠 major outage (broad impact) · 🔴 down (full outage) · 🔵 scheduled maintenance. "Status unknown" means our prober couldn't reach the upstream feed in the last poll — usually transient.

Want a service added? If it has a Statuspage at status.<vendor>.com, send me the URL and I'll add it.
